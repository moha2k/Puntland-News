MADAXWEYNAHA TURKIGA OO BOOQASHO NAADIR AH KU TEGAY DALKA CIRAAQ

Madaxweynaha Turkey Recep Tayyip Erdogan ayaa booqasho naadir ah ku tegay dalka ay deriska yihiin ee Ciraaq halkaasi oo loogu sameeyay soo dhawayn diirran.

Madaxweyne Erdogan ayaa waxaa lagu soo dhoweeyey 21 madaafiic salaan ah oo laga riday garoonka diyaaradaha caalamiga ee Baghdad, halkaasi oo uu ku qaabilay ra’iisul wasaare Mohamed Shia al-Sudani.

Dhinaca kale hoggaamiye Erdogan oo xalay gaaray magaalada Erbil, oo ah caasimadda dawlad-gobolleedka Kurdiyiinta (KRG) ee waqooyiga Ciraaq, ayaa la kulmay Masoud Barzani, oo ahaa madaxweynihii hore ee Kurdiyiinta iyo hoggaamiyaha ugu caansan Kurdiyiinta ee raadinayay inay ka go’aan Ciraaq.

Safarka ayaa imanaya ayada oo ay aad u kacsan tahay xiisadda gobolka ee ka dhalatay dagaalka Israel iyo Xamas ee Marinka Gaza iyo weeraradii ay is-weydaarsadeen Israel iyo Iran.

SALDHIGYO MARAYKANKU KU LEEYAHAY BARIGA SUURIYA OO WEERARO XOOGGAN LA KULMAY

Labada saldhig ee ugu muhiimsan ee Mareykanku ku leeyahay bariga Suuriya ayaa saacad gudaheed dhowr weerar lagu qaaday, sida ay ku warrantay shabakada Al Mayadeen.

Saldhigga ciidanka Mareykanka ee Kharab al-Jir oo ku yaalla waqooyi-bari Suuriya , ayaa lagu qaaday mid ka mid ah weerarradii ugu xumaa ee saldhigga uu la kulmayo.

Wararka ayaa sheegaya in saldhigga iyo hareerihiisa in ay ku dhaceen illaa shan gantaal oo ka mid ah tobannaan gantaal oo ay ku weerarreen kooxaha ay Iran taageerto ee waqooyiga Ciraaq.

Wararkii u horreyay ee warbaahinta ayaa sheegaya in weerarkaasi uu khasaare gaarsiiyay saldhigga.

MADAXWEYNAHA SUURIYA OO DHALIIL XOOGGAN U JEEDIYAY DOWLADDA MARAYKANKA

Madaxweynaha Suuriya, Bashaar Al-Asad ayaa markii u horreysay daah-qaaday in uu kulamo la yeeshay marar badan saraakiil Mareykanka , si dib loogu hagaajiyo xiriirka Mareykanka oo ahaa dalkii u horreeyay ee cunaqabateynta saaray Bashaar, inta uu dalkaa ka socday bannaanbaxyadii looga soo horjeeday ee dhiiga badan uu ku daatay sannadkii 2011.

“Mar walba waxaa jirta rajo, xitaa marka aan ogaano inaysan jirin natiijooyin waa inaan isku daynaa,” ayuu yiri mar la weydiiyay suurtagalnimada in la hagaajiyo xiriirka reer galbeedka.

Laakin taa oo jirta , wuxuu Bashaar Al-Asad ku eedeyay Mareykanka in uu si sharci darro ah ku haysto qayb ka mid ah dhulka Suuriya , isagoo intaa ku daray inaysan jirin natiijo la gaaray oo ka soo baxay kulamada uu la yeeshay Mareykanka si dib loogu hagaajiyo xiriirka diblomaasiyadeed, sida uu ku sheegay waraysi uu siiyay wariye Ruush ah oo ay baahisay wakaaladda wararka Suuriya ee Sana.

Suuriya arrimaha ay hadda isku khilaafsan yihiin Mareykanka waxaa ka mid ah; taageerada Maraykanka ee is maamulka Kurdiyiinta ee waqooyi-bari Suuriya,iyadoo ay Dimishiq ku eedaysay maamulka Kurdiyiinta, oo gacanta ku haya inta badan goobaha saliida Suuriya oo saldhigyo uu ka dhigtay Mareykanka , gooni-goosod iyo koox khaa’inul waddan ah , sababtoo ah xiriirkooda dhow ee Mareykanka.

Si kastaba ha ahaatee, sannadkii hore Suuriya waxay ku soo noqotay ururka Jaamacadda Carabta, iyada oo raadinaysa xiriirka wanaagsan oo ay la yeelato Khaliijka oo xulafo la ah Maraykanka.

Assad ayaa sidoo kale isku dayay inuu hagaajiyo xiriirka uu la leeyahay waddamada kale, isagoo dhawaan booqasho rasmi ah ku tagay Shiinaha markii ugu horreysay muddo labaatan sanno ah. Beijing waxay muddo dheer siin jirtay Dimishiq taageerro diblomaasiyadeed, gaar ahaan Golaha Ammaanka ee Qaramada Midoobay oo ay xubin joogto ah ka tahay.

CATRIONA LAING OO BISHA MAY BANNAYNAYSA XAFIISKA QARAMADA MIDOOBEY U QAABILSAN SOOMAALIYA EE UNSOM

Ergeyga Gaarka ah ee Qaramada Midoobey u qaabilsan Soomaaliya marwo Catriona Laing ayaa muddada xil-haynta Xafiiskaasi soo geba-gebaynaysa bisha May ee sannadkan 2024.

Qoraal ka soo baxay Xafiiska Qaramasa Midoobey u qaabilsan Soomaaliya ee UNSOM ayaa lagu sheegay in Catriona Laing Hawsheeda soo geba-gebanayso bisha May ee soo socota.

“Ergeyga Gaarka ah ee Xoghayaha-Guud ee Qaramada Midoobay u qaabilsan Soomaaliya, Catriona Laing, ayaa soo gebagebeynaysa hawsheeda bisha Maajo” ayaa lagu yiri Qoraalka ka soo baxay UNSOM.

“Waxaan kalsooni buuxda ku qabaa in Soomaaliya ay guuleysan doonto isla markaana Midowga Afrika iyo Qaramada Midoobay, oo ay wehliyaan saaxiibadayada caalamiga ah, ay sii wadi doonaan dowrka muhiimka ah ee lagu taageerayo dadka Soomaaliyeed ee ku socda waddada nabadda iyo xasilloonida.”

“Shakhsi ahaan, Soomaaliya waxay ii tahay goobta rajada iyo saaxiibtinimada, waana sii wadi doonaa taageerada iyo u qareemidda Soomaaliya meel kasta oo aan joogo,” ayaa laga soo xigtay Mw. Laing

ETHIOPIA SEES SEA OUTLET IN SOMALIA AS A PATH TO “ECONOMIC GROWTH”

Abiy Ahmed (right) recognises the mistake of pursuing economic growth through territorial expansionism and ignoring the rules-based global system.

Mogadishu (PP Editorial)— A maritime Memorandum of Understanding agreement signed in January 2024 between Ethiopia’s Prime Minister Abiy Ahmed and Somaliland President Muse Bihi faces challenges. Ethiopia has verbally revised the terms of the deal..

Ethiopia argues that sea access through Somalia’s coastline can benefit both nations. However, this economic focus worries Somaliland’s leadership and sends a mixed message to Somalia’s federal government. Comments by an Ethiopia’s diplomat at a recent security conference (Navigating the Complexities: Fostering Regional Security in the Horn of Africa) in Addis Ababa omitted the diplomatic recognition that Ethiopia would grant to Somaliland as claimed by Muse Bihi as precondition for a naval base for Ethiopia in Somalia’s northern coast.

Ethiopia needs a dedicated port, which could conflict with the economic interests of both Somaliland administration and Djibouti. This subtle expansionist move by Ethiopia requires Western support, similar to what Emperor Haile Selassie received during the annexation of Eritrea by Ethiopia more than six decades ago. The current political situation in Ethiopia is far less stable than it was decades ago.

Ethiopia’s strategy of using economic growth narrative to gain sea access is flawed. Analysts believe the maritime Memorandum of Understanding with the secessionist administration violated international norms, and Ethiopia could face consequences from institutions like the IMF if it disregards the global rules-based system.

“ The emphasis on economic growth for Ethiopia as a means to secure sea access suggests a miscalculation on the part of Abiy Ahmed when he signed a maritime Memorandum of Understanding with the President of the Somaliland administration” said an Ethiopian analyst in Addis Ababa.
